Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the following applications operated by PureReliefLoop: Pure relief and automatisation_music (collectively referred to as "the Apps"). These Apps are automated content publishing tools that publish AI-generated videos on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

1. Information We Collect

The Apps (Pure relief and automatisation_music) do not collect, store, or process any personal data from viewers or users of our content. Our tools operate solely as automated video publishers and do not interact with end-users directly.

2. Use of Platform APIs

Our Apps use official APIs provided by TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram to publish AI-generated video content. We access only the permissions necessary to upload and publish videos on our own channels (video.upload and video.publish scopes). We do not access, collect, or store any data about other users on these platforms.

3. Data Storage

We store only the credentials and tokens necessary to operate our publishing tools on a private, secured server. No user data is collected or stored.

4. Third-Party Services

Our content is generated using AI tools including OpenAI APIs and stock footage from Pexels. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle data.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact
