Last updated: March 24, 2026

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from viewers or users of our content. Our tool operates solely as an automated video publisher and does not interact with end-users directly.

Our tool uses official APIs provided by TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram to publish AI-generated video content. We access only the permissions necessary to upload and publish videos on our own channels. We do not access, collect, or store any data about other users on these platforms.

We store only the credentials and tokens necessary to operate our publishing tool on a private, secured server. No user data is collected or stored.

Our content is generated using AI tools including OpenAI APIs and stock footage from Pexels. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle data.

5. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
